The Venue

Maya Suess

Artist, educator, and arts administrator. Born on a small island off western Canada's coast. BFA from Emily Carr, MFA from Simon Fraser. She makes drawings, installations, videos, performances, and other mischievous entities.

Klovis Gaynor

Musical accompaniment at nearly every edition. Queer musician and "baroque punk" artist. Queens-based. Sometimes joined by Mamie Minch on guitar.

Libby Paloma

Set designer and production support. Queercrip, Mexican-American interdisciplinary artist working in sculpture, installation, and performance. In the business of "world-softening."

The Deal

Sliding scale cover. All monies go directly to the performers. Tickets through Humanitix, where 100% of booking fees go to charity. That's the whole deal.
